# Power-BI-Hospitality-project
# 🏨 Atliq Grands – Hospitality Revenue Analysis Dashboard

---

## 📌 Problem Statement

Atliq Grands owns multiple five-star hotels across India and has been operating in the hospitality industry for the past 20 years.  

Due to strategic moves by competitors and ineffective management decisions, Atliq Grands has been losing market share and revenue in the luxury/business hotel category.

As a strategic initiative, the Managing Director decided to incorporate **Business and Data Intelligence** to regain their market position and revenue growth.  

However, the company does not have an in-house analytics team and therefore decided to hire a third-party service provider to analyze their historical data and generate actionable insights.

---

## 🎯 Task List

As a Data Analyst, the following tasks were performed:

▪ Created metrics as per the provided metric list  
▪ Designed and developed a dashboard based on the stakeholder mock-up  
▪ Generated additional business insights beyond the provided metric list  
▪ Analyzed historical data to identify revenue and occupancy trends  

---

## 📚 Key Learnings from the Project

▪ Built a custom **Calendar Visual** using a Matrix table to analyze time-based trends  

▪ Studied various hotel cancellation policies and understood:
   - Most hotels charge **zero cancellation fee** if cancelled 3 months before the booking date  
   - If cancelled within 3 months, charges range between **60%–90%** of the booking value  

▪ Implemented **Bookmarks and Selection pane** for:
   - Page navigation  
   - Clear filter button  
   - Website-like dashboard experience  

▪ Applied a consistent **color palette strategy** across the dashboard for better visual storytelling  

---

## 📊 Important Business Insights

▪ **Mumbai** generates the highest revenue – ₹669M  
▪ Followed by: Bangalore, Hyderabad, and Delhi  

▪ **AtliQ Exotica** performs best among all 7 property types:
   - Revenue: ₹320M  
   - Rating: 3.62  
   - Occupancy: 57%  
   - Cancellation Rate: 24.4%  

▪ **AtliQ Bay** has the highest occupancy rate – 66%  

▪ **Week 24** recorded the highest revenue – ₹139.6M  

▪ **Delhi** ranks highest in both occupancy and ratings  
   - Followed by Hyderabad, Mumbai, and Bangalore  

▪ Atliq Grands lost approximately **₹298M due to cancellations**  

▪ **Elite room category**:
   - Highest number of bookings  
   - Also has the highest cancellation rate
